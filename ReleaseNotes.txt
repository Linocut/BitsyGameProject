Momokoi 2.0
Release Notes
﻿
Game: Momokoi 
Creator: little_fifth
Version: 2.0  
Date: 1/22/23


New features
* ﻿You can now interact with a magnifying glass and paper inside the kitchen. This will allow you to check which ingredients you need or have from the recipe when you find it. 
* There is now exposition in the beginning to create a narrative. 
﻿
Improvements (of existing features)
* ﻿Walking now uses 1 square instead of multiple to prevent difficulty of moving from place to place. There are also arrows to guide the player to which square they can step on to move. 
* The ending dialog was changed to fit the narrative and the Momokoi’s sound now plays when interacting with it.
